EVENT DETAILS


We’re bringing the Music, Sports and Marketing industries together with the Gaming community over two days LIVE in person and online from Suntec in Singapore.

Gaming & Sports Matters: The worlds of Gaming & Sports have already collided. What happens when they join forces and start to collaborate? Gaming & Music Matters: With artists turning to the metaverse for live shows plus the explosion of virtual artists what else can Gaming offer the music business? Gaming & Marketing Matters: How can brands, gaming companies and creators harness the power of this intersection and fast? And finally… Watch this space for “The Meta Matters”!

Gaming Matters 2021 is a hybrid event. We would love to see you in person if you are in Singapore or you can attend online to network and check out the content live or on demand.

As ever, we are delighted to be hosting Gaming Matters in Singapore, a destination jammed full of wonderful experiences and attractions. Check out the latest programme, book your seat and if you have any questions at all, please contact ami@branded.live who is happy to help.
